Balance bikes teach toddlers the essentials of steering, balancing, and gliding without the distraction of pedals. They build confidence and motor skills far earlier than traditional training-wheel bikes, making them a worthwhile investment for kids aged 18 months to 4 years. When choosing a balance bike, focus on seat-height adjustability (kids should be able to place both feet flat on the ground), overall weight (a lighter bike is easier for little legs to control), and tire type (foam tires are maintenance-free; air tires offer better grip and shock absorption). A simple, well-built bike that fits your child now will serve them better than one packed with extra features.
